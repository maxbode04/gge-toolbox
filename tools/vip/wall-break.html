📊 Result

✓ Validated against real Level 11 Fungal stage-1 and stage-2 clears: the garrison, boss effects (+10,000% wall/gate, +10,100% front/flank, +350% courtyard) and courtyard health all match the game exactly. Calibration finding — a heavily-tooled top hit reduces the boss protection to ~900% net (not zero), which is what makes it a multi-wave grind.

Commander: attack +% boosts your wave strength; defender −% weakens the garrison's combat strength; kill % removes a flat slice of defenders every wave (Tailwhip-style). Dummy waves run first with a token force — they take almost no losses but still fire the kill %, softening the wall before your real waves land.

Model: attacker strength = count × unit attack × (1 + bonuses − boss malus); defender strength = garrison × defence stat × (1 + boss front/flank strength) × (1 − commander defender reduction) ÷ (1 + your front/flank gear) × (1 + max(0, wall/gate protection − your reduction)); each lane (melee vs ranged) resolves separately by attacker share; losses follow (weaker/stronger)1.5; waves hit the surviving garrison sequentially. Walls don't drain boss health — a breach opens the courtyard window. Points = kill share × wall point factor.
