📊 Result — all three segments

✓ Calibrated against two complete Level 11 Mycelial Sovereign clears (stage 1 + stage 2 — all six wall segments). The model reproduces every segment's breach wave and melee losses within ±3%. Four laws the reports made undeniable: a thinning wall dies faster — each wave kills a larger share as the garrison shrinks (so kills rise wave to wave, not flat); ranged take zero wall losses (13,708 ranged on a front, 0 dead); melee absorb the losses, which taper as the wall empties; and tools are consumed per contested wave (none lost after the breach).

Tools & gear: the front is defended by a gate (your melee storms it — rams reduce it, plus your gear's gate reduction) and a wall (your ranged scale it — ladders reduce it, plus gear's wall reduction); each flank is wall-only on both lanes. Reductions stack (tool % × qty) and eat into the boss's +10,000% gate/wall protection. Commander: the five strength stats boost your wave power; each ability carries Toril's real level-3 numbers and only fires on its activation wave — Dragonscale (−15% melee / −30% ranged defence) and Wings (−21% ranged defence, +21% your ranged attack) every 2 waves; Tailwhip (kill 2.5% of the wall, if you outnumber it) every 3 waves; Lasting Wounds (−25% enemy power) over the 2 waves after each 3rd. Because they're cadence-locked, when your real hits land is the whole game — the guide's 4, 8, 10, 14, 16 drops your full army on the waves where the every-2 debuffs and the Lasting-Wounds window all line up. Counts are per lane; dummy waves keep the wave count ticking so the cadences stay aligned.

Model (report-calibrated): attacker strength = count × unit attack × (1 + strengths) × Wings (ranged, when active); total defender strength = remaining garrison × defence stat × (1 + boss front/flank strength) × that wave's abilities × (1 + protection left) × 1.3. Kills per wave = remaining × min(1, (attack ÷ defence)1.5), capped at 66% of the starting garrison so no single wave levels a full wall — as the wall thins the ratio climbs and kills accelerate. Melee losses follow (defence/attack)0.3 against the remaining garrison, so they taper as it empties; ranged never take wall losses. A breach opens the courtyard window. Points = kill share × wall point factor.
