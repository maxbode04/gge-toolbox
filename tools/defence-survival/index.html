🛡️ Defence Survival Calculator

The defender's-seat companion to the Wall-Break sim. Enter your protection, defensive tools and garrison, then the attack heading your way — and see whether each wall segment holds, how many troops you lose, and whether your courtyard falls.

⚠️ Uncalibrated — early build. The combat model uses Goodgame's published loss curve, but the constants haven't yet been tuned against real defence reports. Treat the verdict as a guide, not gospel. If your castle is attacked, send Maxy the full battle report (both sides, all segments) so this can be calibrated like the Wall-Break sim was.
🏰 Your defences

Read these straight off your castle. Wall protects the flanks, the gate protects the front, the moat protects everything. Defensive bonuses come from your castellan, research and decorations.

🧰 Defensive tools

Tools you place on the walls — they raise protection, boost defender strength, or kill attackers outright in the courtyard. Pick up to three.

⚔️ Your garrison

How your defenders are spread across the three wall segments and the courtyard. Melee hold the line; ranged shoot from the wall.

🔥 Incoming attack

What's coming at each segment, and how hard the attacker hits. Rams cut your gate, ladders cut your wall, combo tools sap your defenders' strength.

📋 Verdict

How it reads. Each wall segment is fought wave by wave: your garrison grinds down the incoming army while taking losses of its own. A segment holds if your troops outlast the waves, or is breached if they're wiped — and whatever attackers survive a breach pour into the courtyard for the final stand. Your castle falls only if the courtyard itself is overrun.
